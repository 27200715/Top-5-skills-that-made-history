"The beautiful ball game"

Football, invented around 2000 years ago, in 1863 by
ancient china is a beautiful 11-aside ball game
that had professionally commenced in England. Since
then, without a doubt, football has evolved into
one of the most, if not the most popular sport of
them all. What makes this sport even more spellbinding
is the unbelievable skills the most renowned football
players in the world carry out, going just from simple
body feints, to carrying out a Rainbow Flick, which
is flicking the ball above, and over your head to get
past opponents! With the evolution of the sport, the
number of players, and type of football equipment
have also evolved in an exceedingly positive way!
